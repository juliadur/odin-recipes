Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat or vegetables, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  7. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
